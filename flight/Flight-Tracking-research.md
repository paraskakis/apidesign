# Flight Tracking Public API Market Research

## ICP
Passengers and the general public wishing to stay up to date about the status of a Flight

## Needs & Pain Points
* Plan and anticipate travel time to the airport before departure
* Plan and anticipate travel time to pick up passengers on arrival
* Monitoring Flight Progress to ensure safe arrival of passengers
* Understanding of flight delay patterns and trip time
  
## Benefits

### ICP
* Up-to-date and authoritative information on flight status, from official systems rather than outdated sources, minimizes mistakes.
* Accuracy for trip planning to minimize wasted time and money.
* Free and unrestricted access enables an ecosystem of innovative apps that consumers can use

### Industry
* Reliable, predictable trip information enhances trust and increases the likelihood of loyalty.

## Use Cases

Based on market research, the following use cases have been discovered, in order of popularity:

1. Get the current status of a Flight, including the current position for plotting on a map
2. Get Departure time estimates
3. Get Arrival time estimates
4. Get historical Flight Data
5. Get basic Airport information

**Note Public Access**: All data must be accessible without authentication to ensure ease of use for passengers and agents.
